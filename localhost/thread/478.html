 		So this is currently the thing I'm working on, porting a full set of Super Mario Odyssey themes to replace every theme in SMB. Buntendo's themes are a base for every theme except Water, which I edited on my own. Slightly changed the colour of the backgrounds which will have to do until a reliable and easy background replacement for those themes become mainstream. Original music is in the Cascade and Metro Kingdoms.



Ground: Cascade Kingdom
Changes:
-shrinking and blurring the background so it doesnt clash with the foreground tiles as much
-changng the block tile so it doesnt clash with the semisolids
-replacing the flower decoration with an edited skeleton decoration and making the rock very slightly smaller

Underground: Cap Kingdom
Changes:
-taking elements from Samplasion's Cap Kingdom mod (the pipes, making the doughnut block a hat)
-adding gold to two of the semisolids
-making one mushroom platform a hat and changing the colours of the other two

Water: Lake Kingdom
Changes:
-chaning the floor tiles completely (using the floor of the Cascade Kingdom)
-changing the colours of two of the semisolids to be closer to Lake Kingdom aesthetics
-making one mushroom platform a roof and changing the colours of the other two
-adding ground decoration
-changing the colours of the pipes and blocks

Ghost House: Wooded Kingdom
Changes:
-making one mushroom platform a tree and changing the colours of the others
-making the ground decoration darker

Airship: Metro Kingdom
Changes:
-none because this is flawless

Castle: Luncheon Kingdom
Changes:
-making one mushroom platform the building roof
-making another a corn cob
-making one semi-solid the colours of the hills in the kingdom
-adding a purple pot ground decoration

General Changes:
-changing the colour of every ice block except for the Wooded Kingdom
-squaring off the edges for every standard bridge
-adgusting the background colours for the themes that need it
-making all the used con blocks in line with the one from the original Cap Kingdom mod
-changing all the unchanged doughnut blocks

To Do:
-the rest of the music (I actually need help with this in terms of naming and conversion from WiiU) namely the Cap, Lake and Luncheon Kingdoms.

Example Levels


Just wanted to make a level with each theme to see how it looks in game or give an idea of how to use a few of the things. Of course give feedback on these if you want, though I'd certainly appreciate stars and feedback and all that jazz. But it's mainly visual aid. As an added bit of exploration and puzzle solving, each level has one 1-UP Mushroom to find, though most are hidden.
